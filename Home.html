About Me

I am a 22-year-old Cybersecurity student at the University of Jeddah, currently exploring different areas of information security. My main focus right now is building a strong foundation in network defense and monitoring, with the goal of starting my career as either a SOC Analyst or a Penetration Tester. I am also learning penetration testing tools and techniques to better understand offensive security. Currently, I am preparing for both the CCNA and CompTIA Security+ certifications, and I plan to decide between pursuing SOC or Pentesting as my long-term career path based on hands-on experience and market demand. I am passionate about continuous learning, problem-solving, and contributing to making digital systems more secure.
